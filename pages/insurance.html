Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept my insurance?

We accept most major insurance plans. Please call our billing department at (555) 123-4567 to verify your specific coverage.

What if I don't have insurance?

We offer self-pay discounts and payment plans for uninsured patients. Contact our financial counselors for assistance.

How do I verify my coverage?

Call our insurance verification line at (555) 123-4568 or bring your insurance card to your appointment.

What documents should I bring?

Please bring your insurance card, photo ID, and any referral forms from your primary care physician.
