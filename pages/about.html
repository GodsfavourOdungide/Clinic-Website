Our Story

Founded in 1995, Divine-Favour Medical Center has grown from a small clinic to a premier healthcare facility serving thousands of patients annually. Our commitment to excellence and compassionate care has made us a trusted name in the community.

We combine cutting-edge medical technology with a patient-centered approach to deliver the highest quality healthcare services. Our multidisciplinary team of specialists works together to ensure comprehensive care for every patient.
