Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

You can schedule an appointment by calling our office, using our online booking system, or visiting us in person. Walk-ins are also welcome for urgent care.

We accept most major insurance plans including Medicare, Medicaid, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, Cigna, and UnitedHealthcare. Please contact our office to verify your specific plan.

Please bring your insurance card, photo ID, list of current medications, relevant medical records, and completed new patient forms (available on our website).

Our regular hours are Monday-Saturday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. The emergency department is open 24/7 for urgent medical needs.

You can access your medical records through our secure patient portal. You may also request physical copies from our medical records department.
